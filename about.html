About Me

Hey my name is Vlad Petrosyan! I'm a student at Cannon McMillan High School. I love everything about technolgy, wether its hardware or software I love it all. I just think its so cool that we can create these amazing things with just a few lines of code.

My Bio

I’m a first-generation student from Erie, PA, with a passion for technology that started early. At age 7, I was already setting up Xbox consoles, and by 13, I taught myself to build computers through YouTube tutorials. That same year, I got my first Arduino and began exploring C++.

At 14, I dove into Linux using a Raspberry Pi and Ubuntu, fueling my interest in automation and programming. Eager to challenge myself, I skipped the introductory Python class at school and advanced to the next level. Around this time, I joined an FTC robotics team, where I stepped up as head programmer, learning Java on the fly and helping lead the team to build a competitive robot.

Now at 15, I code in multiple languages, work on robotics, automation, and cybersecurity projects, and am constantly seeking new opportunities to learn, build, and innovate.

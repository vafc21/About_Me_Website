My Computer

I was 13, and I really wanted a gaming computer, but they were so expensive. Then I found out about a local bidding website called Mac Bids. I saved up some money from holidays and birthdays and started placing bids on cheap PC parts.

While doing this, I watched a ton of PC-building YouTube videos. I probably watched one particular two-hour video around six times!

As soon as I got all the parts, I built the computer — and it posted! I was very excited.

The specs for the computer at that time were:

  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 5600
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1080
  • RAM: 16GB DDR4

FTC Robotics 2024-2025

This season I joined the team late. When I arrived, they were really struggling to find a programmer because the head programmer was also trying to manage an FRC team.

When I stepped in and mentioned that I knew some Python, he said, "Perfect, take these courses on Java." So I took the courses, and about a week later I understood most of the code. For the rest of the season, I focused on writing basic OpMode code.

Towards the end of the season, I wanted to write an autonomous mode, but I couldn’t figure out Road Runner at the time since the tuning wasn’t working. Other than that, I think our team performed pretty well.

Overall, I really enjoyed the experience. I’d love to see my code actually do something in real life. I made great friends, and I can’t wait for the 2025 season!

I built the website and also created a JSON file that she can edit herself with titles, images, and other details. Right now, it’s not live because we’re waiting for her license to sell baked goods, but once she gets that, it’s going to be live.

My Computer

I was 13, and I really wanted a gaming computer, but they were so expensive. Then I found out about a local bidding website called Mac Bids. I saved up some money from holidays and birthdays and started placing bids on cheap PC parts.

While doing this, I watched a ton of PC-building YouTube videos. I probably watched one particular two-hour video around six times!

As soon as I got all the parts, I built the computer — and it posted! I was very excited.

The specs for the computer at that time were:

  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 5600
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1080
  • RAM: 16GB DDR4

// About Me

Hey my name is Vlad Petrosyan! I'm currently a Junior at Seneca Valley High School. I love everything about technology. I try to devote as much of my time in high school to understand and program technology. Whether it is a game, software, or a robot I am up for the challage.

// My Bio

I'm a first-generation student from Pittsburgh, PA, with a passion for technology that started early. At age 7, I was already setting up Xbox consoles, and by 13, I taught myself to build computers through YouTube tutorials. That same year, I got my first Arduino and began exploring C++.

At 14, I dove into Linux using a Raspberry Pi and Ubuntu, fueling my interest in automation and programming. Eager to challenge myself, I skipped the introductory Python class at school and advanced to the next level. Around this time, I joined an FTC robotics team, where I stepped up as head programmer, learning Java on the fly and helping lead the team to build a competitive robot.

Now at 16, I code in multiple languages and work on robotics, automation, and software development projects, constantly seeking new opportunities to learn, build, and innovate. Last summer, I attended Pittsburgh Cyber Camp and got hands-on experience with cybersecurity.
